Instructor 

Sarah Collins

Sarah Collins is a passionate and highly skilled educator with over a decade of experience in the tech and design industries. With a diverse background that spans Linux administration, front-end programming, Python development, and graphic design, Sarah has dedicated her career to empowering students with the tools they need to succeed in the modern digital landscape.

Sarah holds a Master’s Degree in Computer Science from the University of California, Berkeley, where she specialized in systems administration and web technologies. She also earned a Bachelor’s Degree in Graphic Design and Digital Media from the Rhode Island School of Design (RISD), where she developed her deep understanding of design principles and creative problem-solving. Her professional journey includes working as a Linux Systems Administrator for a Fortune 500 company, where she managed complex server infrastructures and ensured system security.

She later transitioned into front-end development, collaborating with startups to create intuitive and visually stunning websites and apps. As a Python Django developer, Sarah has built scalable web applications for businesses across industries, combining her coding expertise with her eye for design.  Whether you’re learning Linux administration, mastering front-end design, diving into Django development, or exploring graphic design, Sarah’s step-by-step guidance will help you go from beginner to expert with confidence.
